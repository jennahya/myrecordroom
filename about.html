about this site

It's no secret that I love music, and I love collecting records. There’s something grounding about holding an album in my hands — the artwork, the liner notes, the sense of a whole world the artist built.

This site is my personal archive. It helps me keep track of the records I own, some of the ones I’m searching for, and the little stories that made each album matter. It isn’t complete or perfect, but it captures most of my collection.

Feel free to explore, wander through the shelves, and read the notes I’ve left behind each record. This space is meant to be a quiet room to sit with music.
